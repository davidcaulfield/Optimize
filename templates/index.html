What is Optimize?

"Know what you own, and know why you own it." - Peter Lynch

Just as the quote above states, it is critical that every investor understands why they are holding a certain stock and what purpose that stock has in a portfolio. Optimize aims to help make this process for investors. Investors start by entering an objective, a time horizon, and hypothetical stocks that they want to hold in their portfolio. Optimize will then output details on each individual stock as well as provide an analysis of the portfolio as a whole. You will get to see how your hypothetical portfolio would have performed against the S&P500 in the past. Optimize will then make suggestions on how to imporve your portfolio if determines that the stocks that you hold do not match your stated objective. So what are you wating for???
